甲芝魚精 包裝設計

甲芝魚精為使用純天然生態養殖的虱目魚，具有調節生理機能，促進新陳代謝、增強體力，適合各年齡的營養補給品。
市面上目前的營養補給品包裝大多以具象的食材本身去做設計，考慮到產品主要客群為30歲以上具有一定經濟能力的消費者，或是做為送長輩的養生補品禮盒，對包裝的質感會相對較為注重。
在包裝視覺呈現上，為了和坊間產品做出區隔，選擇抽象的表現手法，以漸層波浪和抽象的魚群，呈現出能量的流動感和虱目魚有活力悠游的畫面，而顏色的選用方面，由於瓶身已經為暖色系的深咖啡色，為了突出包裝的標籤而選用了相對突出的藍色及白色。

Jiazhi fish essence

Jiazhi fish essence uses pure natural ecologically farmed milkfish, which can regulate physiological functions, promote metabolism, and enhance physical strength. It is a nutritional supplement suitable for all ages.
Most of the current nutritional supplement packages on the market are designed with figurative ingredients which are used in the product. Considering that the main customers of the products are consumers over the age of 30 with certain economic abilities, or as gift boxes for health supplements for elders, the packaging is essential to look elegant.
In the visual presentation of packaging, in order to distinguish it from other products on the market, an abstract expression method was chosen, with gradient waves and abstract fish groups, showing the flow of energy and the picture of milkfish swimming vigorously and leisurely. And the choice of color, on the one hand, since the bottle body is already dark brown with warm color, in order to highlight the label of the package, relatively prominent blue and white are chosen.
