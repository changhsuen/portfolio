大王製紙 愛適多紙尿褲

「愛，不嫌多。」
用柔性且深富省思含意，透過將心比心的方式，傳遞給無論是購買者還是使用者，也代表了品牌的初心、用心和貼心。
以功能面來說，也代表了穿上不會覺得很多餘、有異物感，而是像內褲的服貼舒適；另一方面也帶出可容納較多尿量的吸收力。因為有愛，所以互相體諒，互相包容，永遠不嫌多。
聯播網廣告先以蓋板方式呈現，吸引使用者注意，隨後二次觸發至底素材互動，可如抽卡一樣向左右切換廣告素材，同時增加廣告與使用者的互動和興趣。

DAIO PAPER Attento diaper

"Love, never too much."
With soft and deep thinking, through the way of heart-to-heart, it is conveyed to both buyers and users, which also represents the original intention, diligence, and caring of the brand.
In terms of function, it also means that wearing it will not feel redundant and foreign body feeling, but it will be comfortable like underwear. On the other hand, it also brings out the absorption force that can accommodate a larger amount of urine. Because of love, mutual understanding and tolerance are never too much.
Network advertisements are first presented as a cover plate to attract users' attention, and then trigger the interaction of the bottom material, which can switch the advertisement material card left and right, and at the same time increase the interaction and interest of the user.
