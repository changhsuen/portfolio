糧二燒肉

糧二燒肉秉持著「原味食悟 回歸味蕾」的精神，顛覆對燒肉的理解，更貼切瞭解食材本身的味道。

客戶希望以日式書法字體來呈現標準字，經過使用iPad寫出各種書法筆畫和字體，最後挑選出幾組再做排列組合而設計出的標準字，而糧二的「二」刻意的寫得像數字2，並加上印章效果的「燒肉」二字。

Barn No.2 Roast Meat

Barn No.2 Roast Meat adheres to the spirit of "returning to the taste buds with original taste awareness", subverting the understanding of roasted meat, and more closely understanding the taste of the ingredients themselves.

The client wanted to use Japanese calligraphy fonts to present the logotype. After using the iPad to write various calligraphy strokes and fonts, I finally selected several groups of characters and then arranged and combined them to design the logotype. The "two" of Barn No.2 is deliberately written like the number 2. "YakiNiku" with the effect of a seal is added.
