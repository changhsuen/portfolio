維大力 荔枝風味汽水

在保留維大力產品特色的情況下，做出符合現代感的設計，使產品包裝跳脫以往給人復古的印象。

設計上配合維大力特有的褐色瓶身，使用透明底配上原本商標的梅花圖案，並把VITALI的紅色標準字放大，以提升在產品包裝上的識別度。為了表現出荔枝風味汽水，在透明底上加入荔枝和氣泡，營造荔枝投入瓶中的感覺。

Vitali Lychee Soda

Under the condition of retaining the characteristics of the products of Vitali, Create a design that conforms to the modern sense, so that the product packaging can break through the retro impression of the past.

The design matches Vitali's unique brown bottle body, uses a transparent bottom with the plum blossom pattern of the original trademark, and enlarges the red logotype of VITALI to enhance the recognition on the product packaging. To express the lychee flavored soda, add lychees and bubbles on the transparent background to create the feeling of lychees dropping into the bottle.
